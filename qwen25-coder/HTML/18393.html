Accessing the Media Samba Shares

\n

To access the media files stored on our Samba server, follow the instructions below based on your operating system:

\n\n

Windows Users

\n
    \n
  1. Open File Explorer.
    2. \n
  2. In the address bar at the top, type: \\computer\share and press Enter.
    3. \n
  3. You may be prompted to enter a username and password. Use your credentials to access the share.
    4. \n
\n\n

Linux Users

\n
    \n
  1. Open your file manager (e.g., Nautilus, Dolphin).
    2. \n
  2. Click on "Connect to Server" or similar option in the menu or toolbar.
    3. \n
  3. In the server address field, enter: smb://computer/share and click Connect.
    4. \n
\n\n

If you encounter any issues accessing the shares, please contact your IT support team for assistance.
