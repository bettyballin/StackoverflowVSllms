Usability Test Instructions

\n
\n

Welcome to the usability test session. The goal is to identify any problems you might encounter when using our new application.

\n

Please follow these instructions carefully and feel free to take your time, as we are here to understand the process from your perspective.

\n
\n\n

Task 1: Find Your Account Information

\n
\n

Pretend you are trying to find your account information. Please walk me through each step you take, out loud, as if I were guiding you on the phone.
